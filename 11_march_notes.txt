List Comprehension:
-------------------
	>it is a concise(quick) way to create a list.

syntax:
	var=[expression for loop]

tuple:
------
	>it is immutable version of list.
syntax:
	var=(value1,value2,..)
	var=value1,value2,...
	var=tuple()
	etc.

set:
----
	>it is a collection of unique elements.
	>doest not maintain indexing
	>it is unordered
	>it is iterable
	>it is mutable
syntax:
	var={value1,value2,...}
	var=set()
	etc.
