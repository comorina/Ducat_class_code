Inheritance:
------------
	>it is a process of defining a new class by reusing 
behaviour of existing class(es).

		or

	>it is the process of class extension without modifying it.

	>exising class is known as parent/super/base and newly 
created class is known as child/sub/derived class.

syntax:
-------
	class classname(exising classname):
		stmts


types of inheritance:
---------------------
	>single level
	>multi level
	>multiple
	>hybrid(multiple+multi level)
	>hierarchical 

Method Resolution Order(MRO):
-----------------------------
	obj.method() #child-->first parent-->second-->.....
